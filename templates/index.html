Language-Agnostic Wikipedia Content Tagging

No guarantees are made that this tool will be maintained.

This is an experimental tool hosted on Toolforge. No additional personal data is collected by this tool per the Cloud Services Terms of Use.

This tool showcases various language-agnostic content tagging models -- i.e. models that can label Wikipedia content with one or more tags such as quality, topic, geographic region -- developed by the Wikimedia Research team.

Topics

Predict which high-level topics -- e.g., Sports, Medicine -- are associated with a given Wikipedia article.

More info. »

Region

Predict which geographic regions -- e.g., United States, Scotland -- are associated with a given Wikipedia article.

More info. »

Misalignment

Explore the misalignment between reader interest and article quality for any given language edition of Wikipedia.

More info. »

Article Quality

Predict the quality for any Wikipedia article from 0 (no content) to 1 (high quality article for that wiki).

More info. »

Occupation Topic

Identify high-level topics based on Wikidata occupations for any Wikipedia article.

More info. »

Edit Types

Identify high-level edit actions within a Wikipedia article edit.

More info. »
